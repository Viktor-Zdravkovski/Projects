About Main Street

Quality street food with dignity. That's our promise.

Our Story

At Main Street, we believe that fast food doesn't have to mean compromising on quality or taste. We're a local restaurant in Drachevo serving the community with food we're proud of.

Every burger, burrito, and pancake is made with fresh ingredients and prepared with care. Our team is friendly, our kitchen is clean, and our commitment to consistency is unwavering.

We focus on what matters: great taste, generous portions, and service with dignity.

Our Specialties

Perfect Burgers

Juicy beef patties cooked just right, fresh vegetables, and our special house sauce.

Amazing Burritos

Packed with flavor and filling. Our burritos are loaded with quality ingredients wrapped up tight for easy eating.

The Famous 50cm Pancake

Our signature dessert! A massive pancake topped with vanilla and chocolate cream. It's become legendary in Drachevo.
